## KEY SPONSORSHIP SELLING POINTS

1. **ONLY Suzuki in the 250SX West field** -- Crockett is the sole Suzuki RM-Z250 entry in the entire 250 West division. If you see a Suzuki 250 on track Saturday night, it belongs to Crockett Myers. This gives sponsors exclusive brand visibility on a unique platform.

2. **Consistent Main Event qualifier** -- Has made the main event at every 2026 250SX West round, placing him in the Top 16-17 riders with guaranteed TV time every race weekend.

3. **National TV coverage every weekend** -- All 2026 Supercross races air on NBC, Peacock, and NBCSN. The 2026 season opened with record streaming audiences; Race Day Live streaming minutes are up 12% over 2025. The first three rounds were sold out with 124,000+ fans in attendance.

4. **Professional pit setup** -- Maintains a professional pit presence each weekend.

5. **Trajectory is upward** -- Went from LCQ transfers at the start of the season to earning his first-ever direct heat race transfer at Glendale (Round 5). Heat race finishes have improved from LCQ-dependent to 6th and 9th in heats.

6. **Year-round racing** -- Competes in both Supercross (January-May) and Pro Motocross (May-September), giving sponsors roughly 9 months of racing exposure per year, plus SMX Playoffs in September.

## NARRATIVE ANGLES FOR SPONSORSHIP DECK

1. **The Only Suzuki Story:** Crockett is literally the only Suzuki in the 250 class making main events. This is a powerful differentiator -- any sponsor logo on his bike/gear is uniquely identifiable and not lost in a sea of identical bikes.

2. **The Privateer Underdog:** Self-funded/minimally supported rider competing against factory teams with million-dollar budgets. The "privateer grind" story resonates deeply with the motocross fanbase and general sports audiences.

3. **Upward Trajectory:** Clear year-over-year improvement:
   - 2024: Struggled to make mains, best SX finish was 20th
   - 2025: Consistent top-20 finisher, qualified 10th in outdoor MX
   - 2026: Making every main event, cracking the top 15, earning direct transfers

4. **Young & Hungry:** At 22 years old, Crockett is still early in his career with significant upside. Sponsors who invest now get in on the ground floor.

5. **Proven Winner:** Has a professional Main Event victory (2024 Arenacross Daytona) and amateur championship pedigree (Loretta Lynn's moto winner, Mini Os champion, Ponca City champion).

6. **Year-Round Exposure:** Supercross (Jan-May) + Pro Motocross (May-Sept) + SMX Playoffs (Sept) = ~9 months of racing and TV exposure.

7. **Personality & Marketability:** Described by industry media as having "rad style on a bike and positive personality" -- the kind of rider fans and media gravitate toward.
